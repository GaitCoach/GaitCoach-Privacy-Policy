Privacy Policy – GaitCoach
Last updated: February 2026

Privacy Policy:
GaitCoach respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is used when you use the GaitCoach app.

Information We Use:
GaitCoach uses walking-related data from Apple Health, including metrics such as step length, walking speed, asymmetry, and double support time.

This data is used only on your device to:
- Generate summaries of your walking patterns
- Compare your metrics to age- and sex-based population norms
- Display trends and educational insights

Data Storage:
GaitCoach does not store your Apple Health data on external servers.
All walking data is processed locally on your device. GaitCoach does not upload, sell, or share your Health data with third parties.

Personal Information:
GaitCoach asks for limited information such as:
- Year of birth (used to determine age range)
- Sex (used for population comparisons)
This information is stored locally on your device and is used only to improve the accuracy of displayed comparisons.

Third-Party Services:
GaitCoach uses Apple HealthKit to access walking data. GaitCoach does not access any other third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Medical Disclaimer:
GaitCoach is an educational tool only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical concerns.

Your Control:
You can revoke GaitCoach’s access to Apple Health data at any time via:
Apple Health → Sharing → Apps → GaitCoach

Contact:
If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact: gaitcoach@gmail.com
